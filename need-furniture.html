Need Furniture?

Affordable furniture and household essentials for people rebuilding their homes.

Support for people in need

We provide affordable furniture and household essentials to people on low incomes who are referred to us by support agencies and professionals. Our prices are lower than many national charity shops, and any money raised goes straight back into helping others.

Who we help

We support people going through difficult life changes, including homelessness, moving on from supported accommodation, domestic abuse, rehabilitation and other situations where a home needs to be rebuilt from very little.

We aim to offer practical help with dignity, so people can create a safe and settled home.

How to access support

Referral forms are not publicly downloadable and must be requested directly from us by a support agency or professional.

If you already work with a support worker, housing officer, school, charity or health professional, they can contact us on your behalf.
