Practical support with dignity

Turntable Furniture is a local charity helping individuals and families establish or rebuild safe homes during difficult times. We support people facing financial hardship, homelessness, domestic abuse, rehabilitation, moving on from supported accommodation, and other life-changing circumstances.

Local

We focus on Exeter and surrounding communities, providing direct, practical help close to home.

Affordable

Furniture is offered at low cost so households can furnish a home without impossible expense.

Sustainable

We give quality furniture a second life, reducing waste and keeping reusable items out of landfill.

Furniture and items we need most

Good quality donated items help us respond quickly to local need. Clean, reusable household essentials are always appreciated.

  • Sofas and armchairs with fire labels
  • Beds and mattresses with fire labels
  • Wardrobes, drawers and tables
  • Working cookers, washing machines and fridges
  • Microwaves, kettles and toasters
  • Bedding, towels, pots, pans, plates and cutlery

Important for donors

To keep everyone safe and meet regulations, upholstered furniture and mattresses must have fire labels attached. We can also accept working white goods and electrical items, subject to condition.

For referral partners

We work with support agencies, local authorities, charities, schools, health professionals and other frontline services. Referral forms are not available as a public download and must be requested directly from us.

Who can refer?

Referral partners may include support workers, housing teams, domestic abuse services, schools, food banks, health teams and other trusted professionals supporting people in need.

Why support agencies work with us

We offer a practical, local and compassionate service that helps clients move into safer, more settled homes with the essentials many people take for granted. We work alongside support agencies, local authorities, community partners and other frontline professionals who trust us to provide dignified, affordable help when it is needed most.
